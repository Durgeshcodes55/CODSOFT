Posted by Durgesh on Monday, June 21st 2027

Rohit Sharma, the 'Hitman' of Indian cricket, is renowned for his elegant stroke play and ability to score big hundreds. His journey from a talented youngster to a cricketing legend is nothing short of inspiring.

In this post, let's delve into the life and career of Rohit Sharma. We will explore his achievements, records, and what makes him one of the greatest batsmen of all time.

Early Life and Career

Rohit Sharma was born on April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, India. He made his debut for India in 2007 and quickly established himself as a key player in the team.

Over the years, Rohit has broken numerous records and set new standards in cricket. His ability to play long innings and score double centuries in ODIs is a testament to his skill and determination.

Here are some key milestones in his career:

  1. Debut in 2007
  2. First ODI double century in 2013
  3. World Cup triumph in 2019

You can learn more about his career at ESPN Cricinfo

Achievements and Records

Rohit Sharma has a long list of achievements and records to his name. Here are 5 of the most notable ones:

  • Only player to score three double centuries in ODIs
  • Highest individual score in an ODI (264)
  • Leading run-scorer in T20Is
  • Part of the 2011 World Cup-winning squad
  • Named ICC ODI Player of the Year in 2019
